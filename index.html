Stronger Than Titanium

The Nokia 3310 was introduced in September 2000 and discontinued in 2005. This phone was so tough, it could survive anything; in fact, we believe this and the Toyota Hilux bakkie could be the strongest creations of all time.

Reviews

“A phone that just works.” – Tech Enthusiast

“Indestructible and user-friendly. I dropped mine off a building, and the floor cracked.” – Mobile Review

Achievements

  • Sold over 126 million units worldwide—probably because it could survive a nuclear blast.
  • Popularized mobile gaming with the inclusion of Snake, the game that taught us how to multitask while avoiding imaginary walls.
  • Considered a cultural icon and a symbol of early mobile technology—seriously, it’s the phone we all wish we could still use.
